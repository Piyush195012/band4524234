About The Local Train

The Local Train is a rock band known for its soulful music and powerful lyrics. Formed in Chandigarh, the band consists of Ramit Mehra, Sahil Sarin, Raman Negi, and Paras Thakur. They have gained popularity with their albums 'Aalas Ka Pedh' and 'Vaaqif'. Their music resonates with fans across the country, often addressing themes of hope, love, and self-actualization.
